Skyr with Walnuts

Delicious high protein snack!

This healthy recipe is a delicious snack with high protein!
It's quick and easy to make and great for pre and post workout.

Ingredients

  • 40g of walnuts
  • 5g of honey
  • 12ml of linseed oil
  • 200g of skyr
  • 15g of protein complex powder (optional)

Steps

  1. Crush walnuts with your bare hands like a viking
    and put them in a bowl.

  2. Put the honey on top the walnuts

  3. Add the linseed oil

  4. Now put the skyr inside the bowl

  5. Optionally you can add the protein
    complex powder for a little protein boost.
